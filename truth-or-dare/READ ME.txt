Welcome to the superior truth or dare game. 

To start, open the index.html file.

To load the game data, click the 'load file' button in the bottom right, 
locate the data.json file in the root of the file you downloaded, and click open. 
This should automatically load in all the data. 

When your done, make sure to click save to ensure everything is properly stored for later!

If you need a reminder, click the help button in the bottom right
